A Route Between the 10 Best National Parks in the United States

Overview

The United States is one of the largest countries on the planet. This vast area is home to 62 National Parks, which attract hundreds of millions of visitors annually. These parks are scattered throughout the country and all have something special to offer. Nonetheless, I examined the National Parks in the United States that attract the most visitors.

A feature story that was recently released by the magazine hightlighted these top ten most visited parks and the beauty they offer. This web mapping application was designed in order to showcase a route between these parks. The route can start with Acadia National Park or Grand Canyon National Park.

This application shows the route that is neccessary to travel in between all ten of these parks. Hovering over the location markers will display the park name. This route takes approximately 97 hours and spans from coast to coast of the United States.
